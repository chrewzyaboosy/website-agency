Questions

Good questions, straight answers.

How much does a website cost?

Sites start at $149 one-time + $99/mo for the care plan, and a logo, business email & Google setup are included. Most projects land between Starter and Growth. You'll get a clear, fixed price before any work starts — no surprises.

How long until it's live?

Most single-page sites go live within a week — often in just a few days once we have your details. Bigger builds with automation take a little longer, and we'll give you the exact timeline up front.

Do I own my website?

Yes. The site is yours. Your domain stays in your name, and if you ever leave the care plan, you keep your site files. No hostage situations — that's not how we work.

What does ongoing support actually cover?

The monthly care plan covers hosting, security, uptime monitoring, updates, and monthly tweaks — change your hours, swap a photo, add a service, just ask. One dedicated team that knows your site, in plain English. Cancel anytime.

Is SEO included?

Every build ships with the local-SEO foundations: fast load times, mobile-first design, on-page basics, structured data, and Google Business Profile + Maps setup. Want to go after more search terms? The extra pages & SEO add-on covers that.

What do the automation systems actually do?

It answers new inquiries in seconds — even after hours — captures the lead's details, books appointments, sends reminders, and requests reviews after the job. You stop losing customers to whoever replies first.

Do you work with my type of business?

Very likely yes. We focus on local service businesses — contractors, trades, restaurants, gyms, salons, dentists, and similar. If you serve customers in the Milwaukee area, let's talk. Not sure? Ask — the quote is free.
