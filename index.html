Questions

Good questions, straight answers.

How much does a website cost?

Sites start at $149 one-time + $99/mo for the care plan, and most projects land between the Starter and Growth tiers. You'll get a clear, fixed price before any work starts — no surprises, no hourly meter running.

How long until it's live?

Most single-page sites go live within a week — often in just a few days once we have your details. Bigger builds with automation take a little longer, and we'll give you the exact timeline up front.

Do I own my website?

Yes. The site is yours. Your domain stays in your name, and if you ever leave the care plan, you keep your site files. No hostage situations — that's not how I work.

What does ongoing support actually cover?

The monthly care plan covers hosting, security, uptime monitoring, updates, and monthly tweaks — change your hours, swap a photo, add a service, just ask. You've got one person who knows your site and replies in plain English. Cancel anytime.

Do you work with my type of business?

Very likely yes. I focus on local service businesses — contractors, trades, restaurants, gyms, salons, dentists, and similar. If you serve customers in the Milwaukee area, let's talk. Not sure? Ask — the quote is free.
